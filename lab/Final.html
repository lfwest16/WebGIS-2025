This web map visualizes bus route accessibility in Huntersville, North Carolina. The blue points represent designated bus stop locations, while the route lines depict the operational paths of the local transit network. The green buffer zones illustrate the areas of walkable access surrounding each stop, providing insight into how easily nearby residents can reach public transportation. Together, these layers offer a clear assessment of transit coverage and the overall accessibility of bus services within the community.
